INTRODUCTION

It is a well known fact that communication has become an important part of life.With out communication there would not be social, culturale and economical development of a country.

A communication system is a type of stystem in which a data of information is transfered between a sender and reciever using different type of channels. Out of this channel one that is very important is an optical fiber.

What Are Optical Fibers

Optical fiber is the technology associated with data transmission using light pulses travelling along with a long fiber which is usually made of plastic or glass. Metal wires are preferred for transmission in optical fiber communication as signals travel with fewer damages. Optical fibers are also unaffected by electromagnetic interference.

The fiber optical cable uses the application of total internal reflection of light. The fibers are designed such that they facilitate the propagation of light along with the optical fiber depending on the requirement of power and distance of transmission. Single-mode fiber is used for long-distance transmission, while multimode fiber is used for shorter distances. The outer cladding of these fibers needs better protection than metal wires.

Why Are They Important

Optical fibers are prefered due to the following reasons:

  • 1. Faster Internet Speeds
  • 2. Have Wider Coverage
  • 3. Greater and better bandwidth
  • 4. Lower Cost of Ownership
  • 5. Flexibility and Versatility
  • 6. Reliability
